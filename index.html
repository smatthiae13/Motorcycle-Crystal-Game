Motorcycle Collector

You will be given a random number at the start of the game

There are 4 Motorcycles below. By clicking on a Motorcycle you will add a specific amount of point to your total score

You will win the game by matching your total score to random number, you loose the game if your total score goes above the random number.

The value of each Motorcycle is hidden from you until you click on it

Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each Motorcycle.
